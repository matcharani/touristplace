Incredible India - A Land of Diversity

Andhra Pradesh - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit Tirupati Balaji Temple and explore Chandragiri Fort.
  • Day 2: Explore Araku Valley and visit Borra Caves.
  • Day 3: Enjoy the beaches of Visakhapatnam and visit Kailasagiri.

Arunachal Pradesh - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Explore Tawang Monastery and Sela Pass.
  • Day 2: Visit Bumla Pass and Madhuri Lake.
  • Day 3: Explore Dirang Valley and the ancient Dirang Monastery.

Assam - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit Kaziranga National Park and enjoy an elephant safari.
  • Day 2: Explore Kamakhya Temple and visit the tea gardens of Jorhat.
  • Day 3: Discover Majuli Island and the Vaishnavite monasteries.

Bihar - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit Nalanda University ruins and the historical Patna Sahib Gurudwara.
  • Day 2: Explore Bodhgaya and the Mahabodhi Temple.
  • Day 3: Visit Rajgir and Vishwa Shanti Stupa.

Chhattisgarh - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit Chitrakoot Waterfalls and the Kanger Valley National Park.
  • Day 2: Explore the ancient temples of Sirpur.
  • Day 3: Discover the Bastar tribal culture and visit the local markets.

Goa - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Relax on the beaches of North Goa.
  • Day 2: Explore the churches of Old Goa and the spice plantations.
  • Day 3: Enjoy the nightlife in South Goa and visit the Dudhsagar Waterfalls.

Gujarat - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Rann of Kutch and enjoy the cultural festival.
  • Day 2: Explore the Gir National Park and the Somnath Temple.
  • Day 3: Visit the Ajanta and Ellora Caves.

Haryana - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary and explore Pinjore Gardens.
  • Day 2: Explore the Brahma Sarovar in Kurukshetra.
  • Day 3: Discover the Surajkund Mela for local crafts and food.

Himachal Pradesh - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit Shimla and Kufri for scenic views and activities.
  • Day 2: Explore Manali and Rohtang Pass.
  • Day 3: Visit Dharamshala and the Dalai Lama Temple.

Jharkhand - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the famous Ranchi Falls and the Tagore Hill.
  • Day 2: Explore the historical town of Hazaribagh and its wildlife sanctuary.
  • Day 3: Discover the temples and scenic beauty of Deoghar.

Karnataka - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Explore the historic city of Mysuru and visit the Mysore Palace.
  • Day 2: Visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Hampi and its temples.
  • Day 3: Relax on the beaches of Gokarna and visit the Murudeshwar Temple.

Kerala - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Enjoy a backwater cruise in Alappuzha (Alleppey) and visit the Kerala Backwaters.
  • Day 2: Explore the tea gardens of Munnar and visit Eravikulam National Park.
  • Day 3: Discover the historic Fort Kochi and its colonial architecture.

Madhya Pradesh - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the UNESCO-listed temples of Khajuraho.
  • Day 2: Explore the ancient town of Sanchi and its Buddhist stupas.
  • Day 3: Discover the wildlife at Kanha National Park and Bandhavgarh National Park.

Maharashtra - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the famous Gateway of India and Elephanta Caves in Mumbai.
  • Day 2: Explore the Ajanta and Ellora Caves in Aurangabad.
  • Day 3: Relax at the beaches of Alibaug and visit the Murud Janjira Fort.

Manipur - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Loktak Lake and its floating islands.
  • Day 2: Explore the Keibul Lamjao National Park and its biodiversity.
  • Day 3: Visit the Kangla Fort and the Shree Govindajee Temple.

Meghalaya - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Explore the living root bridges and caves in Cherrapunji.
  • Day 2: Visit the Umiam Lake and the Shillong Peak for panoramic views.
  • Day 3: Discover the Mawlynnong village, known as Asia's cleanest village.

Mizoram - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Champhai valley and the Murlen National Park.
  • Day 2: Explore the Soloman’s temple and the Reiek mountain.
  • Day 3: Discover the Pukzing Cave and the Khuangchera Puk.

Nagaland - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Kohima War Cemetery and explore the local markets.
  • Day 2: Discover the cultural festivals of Nagaland, such as the Hornbill Festival.
  • Day 3: Explore the village of Kisama and the surrounding landscape.

Odisha - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Konark Sun Temple and the Puri Jagannath Temple.
  • Day 2: Explore the Chilika Lake and its migratory birds.
  • Day 3: Discover the beautiful beaches of Gopalpur and visit the Raghurajpur artist village.

Punjab - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Golden Temple in Amritsar and the Jallianwala Bagh memorial.
  • Day 2: Explore the wagah border ceremony and visit the Durgiana Temple.
  • Day 3: Visit the city of Patiala and its historical forts.

Rajasthan - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Amber Fort and Hawa Mahal in Jaipur.
  • Day 2: Explore the Udaipur City Palace and Jag Mandir Lake.
  • Day 3: Discover the Jodhpur Mehrangarh Fort and the blue city.

Sikkim - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit Tsomgo Lake and the Baba Mandir.
  • Day 2: Explore Pelling and visit the Pemayangtse Monastery.
  • Day 3: Trek to the Yumthang Valley and discover the hot springs.

Tamil Nadu - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 3: Discover the hill station of Ooty and visit the botanical garden.

Telangana - 3 Day Plan

Tripura - 3 Day Plan

Uttar Pradesh - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Taj Mahal in Agra and the Agra Fort.
  • Day 2: Explore the ancient temples of Varanasi and the Ghats.

Uttarakhand - 3 Day Plan

West Bengal - 3 Day Plan

  • Day 1: Visit the Howrah Bridge and Victoria Memorial in Kolkata.

  • Day 3: Discover the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway and the tea gardens.
